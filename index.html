About the Rail Line Project

RailLink AUS is working to establish a direct rail service between Adelaide, SA and Mildura, VIC. Our vision is to boost regional connectivity, support local economies, and provide a sustainable transportation solution.

Interested in supporting or partnering? Contact us to get involved!

About Us

Founded by an Australian rail enthusiast, RailLink AUS seeks to unite communities and businesses across South Australia and Victoria. This project is at the concept stage and open for discussions with stakeholders, investors, and the community.
